I wear it.
The face of happiness,
while it boils beneath.
The animated mask that covers that numb froth.

"Ha ha ha!"
Nothing.

But the cracks show.
The pressure grows.
Pushing. Forcing.
I boil. I break.

I vent.
Empty.

The mask is restored.

The pressure builds.
