About Me
I'm a theoretical physicist turned programmer, living and working in London, UK. I'm comfortable working on any aspect of a project, and am always trying learn new skills, and improve on those I already have. I'm currently focusing on game development, wrangling with Unity3D on a daily basis.

I work for a startup called MakieLab. We create toys using game technologies and our first product is a customizable, posable doll, called a Makie, designed by you and 3D printed on demand - a working demo, already on the shelves of Selfridges! We are currently developing a tablet game set in the world of the Makies, which will tie the physical toy into a rich digital world, creating a truly connected play experience.

When I'm not coding, I practice Taekwondo, dabble in photography, and write collaborative fiction online.
